Our Products

We specialize in exporting premium palm tree sweet products. Below are some of our featured products:

Jaggery Powder

Our Jaggery Powder is a 100% natural and unrefined sweetener made from high-quality palm sap. It is an excellent alternative to refined sugar, packed with essential nutrients like iron, calcium, and magnesium.

Palm Sugar Candy

Our Palm Sugar Candy is a delicious, all-natural sweet treat made from the sap of palm trees. It offers a rich caramel flavor and is packed with essential minerals like iron, magnesium, and potassium.

Palm Jaggery

Our Palm Jaggery is a premium, unrefined sugar derived from the sap of palm trees, offering a natural and wholesome alternative to processed sugar. It is rich in iron, calcium, and antioxidants.

Dry Ginger Palm Candy

Our Dry Ginger Palm Candy is a blend of the aromatic zing of ginger and the natural sweetness of palm sugar. It is perfect for digestion, immunity boosting, and serves as a wholesome, healthful treat.
