About Us

Vetrivel Internationals is a trusted name in the export industry, specializing in high-quality palm tree sweet products. We take pride in offering a variety of natural and unrefined sweeteners sourced from the best suppliers, ensuring quality and authenticity in every shipment.

Who We Are

We are a dedicated export company with a strong focus on delivering premium palm-based sweet products to global markets. While we are not manufacturers, we work closely with reliable and certified producers to bring you the finest products available.

What We Offer

Our primary expertise lies in exporting palm tree sweet products such as Jaggery Powder, Palm Sugar Candy, Palm Jaggery, and Dry Ginger Palm Candy. We also cater to custom export needs, ensuring timely delivery through a strong and efficient supply chain.

Why Choose Us?

  • Reliable sourcing from trusted manufacturers
  • Premium quality with strict quality control
  • Customized exports based on customer needs
  • Commitment to global standards and satisfaction

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between high-quality traditional sweet products and global markets, ensuring authenticity, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.
