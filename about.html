About Us

Welcome to Vetrivel Internationals, a trusted name in the export industry, specializing in high-quality palm tree sweet products. We take pride in offering a variety of natural and unrefined sweeteners sourced from the best suppliers, ensuring quality and authenticity in every shipment.

Who We Are

Vetrivel Internationals is a dedicated export company with a strong focus on delivering premium palm-based sweet products to global markets. While we are not manufacturers, we work closely with reliable and certified producers to bring you the finest products available.

What We Offer

Our primary expertise lies in exporting palm tree sweet products such as Jaggery Powder, Palm Sugar Candy, Palm Jaggery, and Dry Ginger Palm Candy. In addition, we cater to customized export requests, providing a wide range of quality products based on customer preferences. Our strong supply chain ensures timely delivery and premium quality every time.

Why Choose Us?

  • Reliable sourcing from trusted manufacturers
  • Premium quality products with strict quality control
  • Ability to export various products based on customer needs
  • Commitment to customer satisfaction and global standards

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between high-quality traditional sweet products and global markets, ensuring authenticity, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.
